# Decision matrix — 3D concrete printer comparison

Weighted scoring matrix to compare four vendors across the criteria that matter most for a Sydney housing pilot.

---

## Scoring criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Why it matters |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| **AU availability** | 25% | Immediate deployability — no import delays, local support |
| **Regulatory precedent** | 20% | Existing NCC compliance path reduces certifier risk |
| **Build envelope** | 15% | Single-piece vs segmented construction cost |
| **Print speed** | 10% | Affects project timeline and crane/crew costs |
| **Entry cost** | 15% | Capex constraint for first pilot |
| **Material compatibility** | 10% | Standard concrete vs proprietary mix requirements |
| **Support / training** | 5% | Onboarding effort and troubleshooting capability |

---

## Raw scores (1–5, 5 = best)

| Vendor | AU avail | Reg precedent | Envelope | Speed | Entry cost | Material | Support | **Weighted** |
|--------|----------|---------------|----------|-------|------------|----------|---------|:------------:|
| **Contour3D** | 5 | 5 | 4 | 3 | 5 (no capex) | 5 | 5 | **4.70** |
| **Luyten 3D** | 5 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 3 ($300–500k) | 4 | 4 | **3.85** |
| **CyBe** | 4 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 5 (€49k) | 4 | 3 | **3.35** |
| **PaCompatible** | 3 | 2 | 3 | 5 | 2 ($519k) | 3 | 3 | **2.95** |

### Calculation example (Contour3D)

```
(5×.25) + (5×.20) + (4×.15) + (3×.10) + (5×.15) + (5×.10) + (5×.05)
=  1.25  +  1.00   +  0.60   +  0.30   +  0.75   +  0.50   +  0.25
= 4.70
```

## Recommended order

| Priority | Vendor | Rationale |
|----------|--------|-----------|
| 🥇 **1st** | **Contour3D** | Zero capex, existing compliance path, local partner, proven house |
| 🥈 **2nd** | **CyBe** | Cheap R&D printer for workshop experimentation and mix trials |
| 🥉 **3rd** | **Luyten 3D** | Good production platform if in-house printing scales |
| 4th | **PaCompatible** | Competitive on speed but expensive; wait for local demo |

---

## Risk assessment

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Contour3D partnership unavailable | Medium | High | Pre-approach with specific pilot proposal |
| NCC certifier unfamiliar with 3DP | High | High | Engage Warne Properties / early certifier involvement |
| Concrete mix fails structural tests | Medium | High | Pre-test at ULTRA Labs before committing to print |
| Printer delivery delays (imported) | Low–Med | Medium | Prioritise Contour3D (local) for pilot |
| Labour shortage (post-print finishing) | Medium | Low | Include finishing budget in financial model |

---

## How to use this

1. Update scores as you get real quotes and test results
2. Re-weight criteria to match your pilot's specific priorities
3. Re-run the weighted calculation for each iteration
