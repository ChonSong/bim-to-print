## Key NCC clauses for 3D-printed concrete

| Clause | Subject | Why it matters |
|--------|---------|----------------|
| **H1P1** | Structural adequacy | Printed walls must meet structural performance |
| **H1D4** | Structural robustness | Deformation, load paths in monolithic printed walls |
| **H2P1** | Fire resistance | Fire-resistance level (FRL) for printed walls |
| **H2D3** | Fire spread | Compartmentation in printed structures |
| **H3P1** | Weatherproofing | Moisture ingress in layered print surfaces |
| **H3D2** | Water penetration | Inter-layer adhesion affects water tightness |
| **H4P1** | Energy efficiency | Thermal mass of printed walls, U-values |
| **H4D3** | Thermal bridging | Continuous insulation in monolithic printed walls |
| **H5P1** | Sound insulation | Acoustic performance of printed wall assemblies |
| **H5D2** | Airborne sound | Inter-tenancy sound in multi-unit printed builds |
| **J1P2** | Energy efficiency compliance | Verification method for printed wall thermal performance |

> **Note:** These clauses reference NCC 2022 Volume Two. Verify exact clause numbering for your jurisdiction (NSW may have variations).
